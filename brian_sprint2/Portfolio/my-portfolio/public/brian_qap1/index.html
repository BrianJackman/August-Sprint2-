A Breif history of the 370Z

After Nissan ceased production on the 300ZX in 1996 they needed a new performance car in North America. After showing off a concert 240Z in 1999 they quickly realized that people still wanted a Z car. The Nissan 350Z was released in 2002 which was followed by the release of the 370Z in 2009 which continued its production run for 11 years before being discontinued in 2020
